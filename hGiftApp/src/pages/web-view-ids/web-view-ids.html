You can use these Gift URIs with the Hybrid Gifting app. To use them you need to encode these as URLs in QR codes and NFC tags.

You can find many QR code generators on the internet that will provide the QR codes as images yo can then print on your media, e.g. stickers. If you have many QR codes to make look to batch functions.

There are many apps on Google Play you can use to encode NFC tags. The Gift URIs should be encoded as NDEF URLs.
